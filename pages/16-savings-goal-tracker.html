Smart Savings Strategies

Pay Yourself First

Automatically transfer a portion of your income to savings before spending on other expenses.

Set SMART Goals

Make goals Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound for better success.

Use the 50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment.

Round Up Purchases

Use apps that round up your purchases to the nearest dollar and save the difference.

Create Milestones

Break large goals into smaller, achievable milestones to stay motivated.

Review & Adjust

Regularly review your goals and adjust contributions based on changing circumstances.
