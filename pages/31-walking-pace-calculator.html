Walking Tips & Guidelines
  • Beginner: Start with 15-20 minute walks at a comfortable pace (15-17 min/mile)
  • Moderate: Aim for 30-45 minutes at 13-15 min/mile pace
  • Advanced: Challenge yourself with 60+ minutes at 12 min/mile or faster
  • Form: Keep your head up, shoulders relaxed, and arms swinging naturally
  • Footwear: Wear comfortable, supportive walking shoes with good arch support
  • Hydration: Drink water before, during, and after your walk, especially for longer distances
  • Progression: Increase distance or pace gradually, no more than 10% per week
  • Recovery: Include rest days and listen to your body for signs of overtraining
