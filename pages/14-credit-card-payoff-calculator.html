Smart Payoff Strategies

Avalanche Method

Pay off cards with the highest interest rates first. This saves the most money on interest over time.

Snowball Method

Pay off the smallest balances first for quick wins and motivation to continue paying off debt.

Set Target Dates

Work backwards from your target payoff date to determine the monthly payment needed.

Extra Payments

Even small extra payments can significantly reduce your payoff time and total interest.

Balance Transfer

Consider transferring high-interest balances to cards with 0% introductory APR offers.

Track Progress

Monitor your debt reduction progress to stay motivated and on track with your goals.
