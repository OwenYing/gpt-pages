Budgeting Best Practices

50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment.

Envelope Method

Use physical or digital envelopes to allocate specific amounts to each spending category.

Zero-Based Budgeting

Every dollar has a purpose - income minus expenses equals zero at the end of each month.

Track & Adjust

Regularly review your budget and adjust categories based on your actual spending patterns.
