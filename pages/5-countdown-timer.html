About Countdown Timers

Countdown timers are essential tools for building anticipation, managing time, and staying focused on important events. Whether you're counting down to a vacation, deadline, or special occasion, this timer helps you visualize the time remaining and stay motivated as the big day approaches.

Features

Multiple Timer Modes: Set custom countdowns or use preset timers for common activities

Visual Progress: See your countdown progress with an animated circular progress bar

Event Management: Save and manage multiple countdown events

Notifications: Get alerts when your countdown reaches zero

Responsive Design: Works perfectly on all devices
