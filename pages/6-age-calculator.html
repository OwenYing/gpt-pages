Fun Age Facts

  • Your age in seconds is constantly changing - every second counts!
  • By age 25, you've lived approximately 788,400,000 seconds
  • The average person lives about 2.5 billion seconds (80 years)
  • Your heart beats about 2.5 billion times in an average lifetime
  • You blink approximately 400 million times by age 20
