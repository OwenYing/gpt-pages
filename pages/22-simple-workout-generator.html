Workout Tips

  • Warm Up: Always start with 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching
  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weight or reps over time
  • Rest Days: Allow 1-2 days between working the same muscle groups
  • Form First: Focus on proper technique before increasing intensity
  • Hydration: Drink water before, during, and after your workout
  • Listen to Your Body: Stop if you feel pain or excessive fatigue
