Reading Tips & Strategies
Preview the Text
Scan headings, subheadings, and first sentences to get an overview before reading.
Active Reading
Take notes, highlight key points, and ask questions while reading to improve comprehension.
Set Time Goals
Break long texts into manageable chunks with specific time targets for each section.
Use Bookmarks
Mark important sections and return to them later for deeper understanding.
Practice Regularly
Regular reading practice improves both speed and comprehension over time.
Take Breaks
Short breaks every 20-30 minutes help maintain focus and comprehension.
