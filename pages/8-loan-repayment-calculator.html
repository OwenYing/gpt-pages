About Loan Repayment

Calculate your monthly loan payments, total interest costs, and view a complete amortization schedule. This tool helps you understand the true cost of borrowing and plan your repayment strategy.

Loan Repayment Tips

  • Shorter Terms: Lower total interest but higher monthly payments
  • Longer Terms: Lower monthly payments but higher total interest costs
  • Extra Payments: Even small additional payments can significantly reduce total interest
  • Refinancing: Consider refinancing if rates drop significantly
  • Prepayment Penalties: Check if your loan has early repayment fees
  • Debt-to-Income: Keep total debt payments under 43% of your income
