About Hydration Tracking

Track your daily water consumption and stay hydrated with personalized hydration goals. Monitor your intake, set reminders, and maintain optimal hydration levels for better health and performance.

Hydration Tips

  • Daily Goal: Aim for 8-10 glasses (64-80 oz) of water per day
  • Activity Level: Increase intake during exercise and hot weather
  • Body Weight: Drink half your body weight in ounces daily
  • Urine Color: Light yellow indicates good hydration
  • Morning Hydration: Start your day with a glass of water
  • Electrolytes: Consider sports drinks for intense workouts
