Understanding Filing Status

Filing Status Definitions:

  • Single: Unmarried individuals who don't qualify for another filing status
  • Married Filing Jointly: Married couples filing together, usually results in lower taxes
  • Married Filing Separately: Married couples filing separate returns
  • Head of Household: Unmarried individuals who pay for more than half the cost of keeping up a home for a qualifying person

Tax-Saving Tips

Maximize Retirement Contributions

Contribute to your 401(k) or IRA to reduce taxable income and save for the future.

Health Savings Account

Consider an HSA if eligible - contributions are tax-deductible and withdrawals for medical expenses are tax-free.

Education Credits

Take advantage of education tax credits if you're paying for qualified education expenses.
