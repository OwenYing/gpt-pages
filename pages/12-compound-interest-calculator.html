About Compound Interest

Compound Interest Tips

  • Start Early: Time is your greatest ally in compound interest
  • Higher Rates: Even small rate increases compound significantly over time
  • Regular Contributions: Adding money regularly accelerates growth
  • Compounding Frequency: More frequent compounding = faster growth
  • Tax Considerations: Tax-deferred accounts maximize compounding benefits
  • Inflation Impact: Consider real returns after inflation
