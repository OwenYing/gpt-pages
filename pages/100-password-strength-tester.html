Why Strong Passwords Matter

Strong passwords are your first line of defense against cyber attacks. Weak passwords can be easily guessed or cracked by automated tools, putting your personal information, accounts, and digital security at risk.

Password Security Best Practices:

  • Length: Use at least 12 characters for maximum security
  • Complexity: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Uniqueness: Never reuse passwords across different accounts
  • Regular Updates: Change passwords periodically, especially for important accounts
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Enable 2FA whenever possible for additional security
