Interesting Facts
Most Abundant Element
Hydrogen makes up about 75% of the visible universe's mass and is the primary component of stars.
Essential for Life
Carbon is the basis for all known life forms and forms the backbone of organic chemistry.
Rare Earth Elements
Despite their name, lanthanides are not particularly rare, but they are difficult to separate from each other.
Noble Gases
These elements are called 'noble' because they rarely react with other elements, similar to how noble people avoid common folk.
Radioactive Elements
All actinides are radioactive, with uranium and thorium being the most common naturally occurring ones.
Metalloids
These elements have properties intermediate between metals and nonmetals, making them useful in semiconductors.
