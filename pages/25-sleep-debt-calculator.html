Sleep Health Tips

  • Sleep Duration: Adults need 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night
  • Sleep Debt: Missing sleep accumulates and affects cognitive function
  • Recovery: It takes multiple nights of good sleep to recover from sleep debt
  • Consistency: Maintain regular sleep and wake times
  • Environment: Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet
  • Technology: Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime
