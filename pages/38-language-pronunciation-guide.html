Pronunciation Tips
Listen Carefully
Pay attention to the rhythm, stress, and intonation patterns of native speakers.
Practice Speaking
Repeat words and phrases aloud to improve your pronunciation and muscle memory.
Learn Phonetics
Understanding phonetic symbols helps you pronounce words correctly without hearing them.
Speak with Natives
Practice with native speakers to get feedback and improve your accent.
Watch and Listen
Watch movies, TV shows, and videos in your target language to hear natural speech.
Sing Along
Singing songs in your target language helps with rhythm and pronunciation.
