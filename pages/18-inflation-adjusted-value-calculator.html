Understanding Inflation

What is Inflation?

Inflation is the general increase in prices over time, which decreases the purchasing power of money.

Impact on Savings

Money saved today will buy less in the future due to inflation. Consider inflation when planning long-term savings.

Investment Strategy

Investments should aim to outpace inflation to maintain or increase real purchasing power over time.

Planning for Retirement

Factor in inflation when calculating retirement needs. $1 million today won't have the same purchasing power in 20 years.

Salary Negotiations

Annual raises should at least match inflation to maintain your real income level.

Hidden Costs

Inflation affects not just prices but also interest rates, investment returns, and economic policies.
