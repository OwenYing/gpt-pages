Step Goal Tips

  • Daily Target: Aim for 10,000 steps per day for general health
  • Gradual Increase: Start with 5,000 steps and build up gradually
  • Activity Level: More active lifestyles may need 12,000-15,000 steps
  • Walking Pace: Brisk walking (3-4 mph) provides better health benefits
  • Break It Up: Multiple short walks throughout the day count just as much
  • Track Progress: Monitor weekly averages to see improvement trends
